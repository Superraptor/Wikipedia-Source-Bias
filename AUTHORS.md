## Upstream and dependencies

This project builds on Wikimedia infrastructure and open source work,
including the Wikimedia [Codex](https://doc.wikimedia.org/codex/) design
system, Wikidata, the Wikipedia API, Crossref, and Media Bias/Fact Check.
It is deployed on [Wikimedia Toolforge](https://toolforge.org).
